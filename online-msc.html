Online M.Sc. course in India

The Online M.Sc. course is a great option for those candidates who want to pursue Masters in Science after B.Sc. but don’t have time to take regular classes due to strict work schedules, financial matters, or even personal reasons. Online M.Sc. program gives the flexibility where candidates don’t need to take regular classes for completing the degree.
The specializations offered in the online MSc program are Data Science, Computer Science, Information Technology, Mathematics, and Business Analytics and each specialization aims at providing theoretical as well as practical knowledge of the selected domain. This program prepares students to get into desired job positions and establish successful careers across industries.

Eligibility for Online M.Sc. Course

Candidates must have completed graduation in any discipline from a recognized University with at least 45–50% of the possible marks. However, the minimal qualifying requirements or eligibility vary depending on the specialization and University selected.

What is the Online M.Sc. Duration?

The duration of an online M.Sc. course is 2 years and maximum it can be completed in 4 years. There are 4 semesters in the curriculum, with 2 semesters per year.

What is the Online M.Sc. Admission Procedure?

The online admissions process for the online M.Sc. in India is made to be as simple as possible.

  • 1

    Open the official website, and register yourself there by providing the basic information such as your name, email address, and contact information.

  • 2

    Create a student login with a username and password to confirm your enrollment.

  • 3

    As directed, enter the necessary information and upload the appropriate documents to register for online M.Sc. courses in India.

  • 4

    Pay the online M.Sc. course fees using one of the many accessible payment methods.

  • 5

    The University will verify the information and send you a confirmation email and message.
