Online B.Sc. Program in India: Overview

Online Bachelor of Science is a degree course after 10+2 that deals with subjects of science such as physics, chemistry, computer science, mathematics etc. Online B.Sc. program gives the flexibility where candidates don’t need to take regular classes for completing the degree which becomes a major help for students.

Various renowned universities in coordination with various online platforms provide BSc online courses. BSc Online course fees range from INR 12,000 to INR 1,00,000. The duration of an online B.Sc. course is 3 years and maximum it can be completed in 6 years. There are 6 semesters in the curriculum, with 2 semesters per year.

Eligibility for Online B.Sc. Course

Candidates must have completed 10+2 in any discipline from a recognized University with at least 45–50% of the possible marks. However, the minimal qualifying requirements or eligibility may vary depending on the specialization and University selected.

Why Study B.Sc. Online?

There are various advantages for which a candidate should go for a BSc degree in an online mode:

For Working professionals

The major reason behind candidates opting for BSc online courses is to maintain their job and studies simultaneously. Candidates who are working professionals, and cannot attend regular colleges; can pursue the online courses to continue their education.

Saves Travel Expenses

Pursuing BSc degree online means you don’t have to go anywhere to attend a class physically; rather you can do the same at home or office itself, which saves your too much time and expenses on traveling.

Works on Self-Pace

Candidates pursuing online BSc degree get the opportunity of learning the subject at their own pace and convenience. They don’t get the pressure of attending a class at a particular time; rather they can watch the recorded session at their convenient time.

Low Cost

Candidates looking to pursue a BSc degree but not be able to do the same offline because of the high fees charged by the colleges. They can go for the online BSc program that is very cost effective as compared to regular colleges.

Highly Experienced Faculty

Faculties that take online BSc classes are highly experienced and they consistently engage with multiple corporate partners to design an industry relevant curriculum, so that students can advance their knowledge through the programme, and become job-ready on multiple industry projects.
