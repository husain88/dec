University Accreditations & Recognitions

Datta Megha Institute of Medical Sciences

The Datta Meghe Institute of Higher Education & Research (Deemed to be University) has a 165-acre campus around 80 km from Nagpur, Maharashtra, India, which is the geographic center of the country. It is located in a quiet neighborhood away from the hustle and bustle of the city in a self-sustaining complex with a wonderful academic atmosphere.

Datta Meghe Institute of Medical Sciences was granted the status of Deemed to be University by University Grants Commission, on 24th May 2005. The University is located in a 125-acre campus in Sawangi (Meghe), Wardha about 80 km from Nagpur, Maharashtra.

The University has been accredited with “A+” Grade by the NAAC & was placed in ‘Category-I Deemed to be University’ as per UGC. The University also received the prestigious Dr. B.C. Roy Award for Institutional Research.
