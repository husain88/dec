Chandigarh University Online Programs

Start your transformative academic journey with Chandigarh University’s prominent online programs. Online university in Chandigarh offers a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate courses.

For aspirants to advance their careers, UGC approved Chandigarh University online offers Online MBA, M.COM, M.SC (DS), MAJM, BBA, BCA, and BAJM

Chandigarh University online was situated in Punjab which was established in 2012. CU is UGC-DEB approved and it has been accredited by NAAC with A+ Grade.

The university has been awarded as Asia’s fastest-growing private institution and recorded in Limca book of records for inviting most numbers of companies in a single year for placement.

Features of the Online Programs for CU-ONLINE

  • Chandigarh University's online courses are made to help students acquire the abilities and skills required to face challenges. Students receive a syllabus outlining the updated curriculum which is aimed to help them keep pace with advances in technology, business management techniques, and hands-on projects.
  • Students have access to recorded classes in case they are unable to attend the live session for whatever reason. Students can use desktop, laptop, smartphone, or mobile phone to attend the lectures at their convenience, based on their schedules. Students can only access assignments, practice questions, mock exams, and other materials via their LMS.
  • Students at the Chandigarh University online can also benefit from a career support programme that offers personality development, interview coaching, resume assistance, and communication skills.
