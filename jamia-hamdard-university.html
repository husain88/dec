Jamia Hamdard University Online Programs

Jamia Hamdard is a deemed to be university established in 1989 in New Delhi, India. It is a government-funded university. In 2019 it was awarded the Institute of Eminence status by Ministry of Human Resource Development. Jamia Hamdard is a UGC recognized university & accredited Grade ‘A’ by NAAC. It was ranked 37TH in the overall rankings of 2020 by the NIRF. The vision of the university is to provide international quality higher education to their students.

Under Graduate Programmes offered by the University

Online BBA (Bachelors of Business Administration)

BBA is a 3 year degree program consists of six semesters. The course is designed to provide training & education that meet the challenges of the evolving global scenario and changing environment in business administration.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 pass or equivalent from a recognized board

Fees Structure

  • 14,000/- Per Semester (6 semesters)
  • Total: 84,500/- for 3 years (includes 500/- Application Fee One Time)
Bachelor of Commerce (B.com)

B.COM is also a three-year undergraduate course that is divided into six-equal semesters. This course teaches you the concepts of financial accounting, taxation, business laws, and others.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 pass or equivalent from a recognized board

Fees Structure

  • 9,000/- Per Semester (6 semesters)
  • Total: 54,500/- for 3 years (includes 500/- Application Fee One Time)
Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)

BCA is a 3 year degree program consists of six semesters. The course is designed to provide the knowledge to all aspects of computer applications, software, computing language, and so on.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 pass or equivalent from a recognized board

Fees Structure

  • 16,000/- Per Semester (6 semesters)
  • Total: 96,500/- for 3 years (includes 500/- Application Fee One Time)
