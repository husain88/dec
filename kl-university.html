KL Deemed to be University is a high-performing university that has been granted autonomy by DHE (Department of Higher Education) under the Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD). They are in control that KL University online degree, programs, curriculum, application and admission process, meet with the specified guidelines.

About The University

KL (Deemed to be University) was established in 1980-81 as KL College of Engineering and attained autonomous status in 2006. It was conferred with Deemed University (Koneru Lakshmaiah Education Foundation Deemed to be University) status in the Year 2009, under section 3 of the UGC act 1956.

KL (Deemed to be University) has been granted Category 1 Status, accorded with A++ Grading earlier by NAAC, and a score of 3.57/4 by UGC. It's among the best results obtained by any school in the nation. In 2019, UGC and MHRD declared this institution as a Category 1 Institution.

The trend of online undergraduate (UG) programs in India is rapidly growing.
