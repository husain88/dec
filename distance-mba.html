The Distance MBA program, a two-year management study course or MBA degree correspondence course in India, is perfect for those who can’t make it to regular classes – and that’s why students love it. The instructional delivery for these courses adopts a hybrid format, with the possibility of weekend visits to the institute or designated centers.
