GLA University Online Programs

GLA University was established in 1991 with a vision to provide quality education to the people. GLA Online University has received all the major approvals from bodies like UGC, AICTE, BCI etc. The university is offering UG, PG and diploma courses through online mode. Today, GLA University is a renowned institute of higher education in North India. GLA Online also has been recognized by the Government of Uttar Pradesh. It is a 12th Private University in India to receive 12B Status.

Placement Assistance

One of the major factor students should consider while choosing any university is the placement assistance, GLA Online University has made partnerships with a number of well-known corporate firms and organizations such as Amazon, Honda, Jindal, Accenture, etc., which provides students with a good professional exposure and opportunities to find good placements after completing their education.

Under Graduate Programmes offered by the University

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

The BBA course is a 3-year degree course offered by GLA Online at the undergraduate level that focuses on imparting the basics of business administration.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Candidate should have completed their Higher Secondary (HSC) level i.e. 10+2 education (Any Discipline)
  • Candidate should have scored a minimum of 50% Aggregate marks
  • Students from SC/ST/OBC or Any Other Reserved Categories should have Scored at least 45% Marks at HSC level

Career Prospect after doing BBA

  • Finance Manager
  • Business Manager
  • Administration Manager
  • Human Resource Manager
  • Research and Development Manager
  • Business Consultant
  • Information Systems Manager
  • Marketing Manager
  • Insurance Manager
  • Operations Manager

Duration

  • Minimum 3 years (6 semesters) and Maximum time duration to complete the program – 8 years

Specialization

  • Marketing Management
  • Finance Management
  • Human Resource Management
  • Banking and Insurance Management

Fees Structure

  • Fee Year Wise: 20000/- per year
  • Registration & Alumni fee: 1000/- each (at the time of admission)
  • Total Course fee: 62000/-
