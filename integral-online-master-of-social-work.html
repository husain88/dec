Online Master of Social Work (MSW)

Master of Social Works degree focuses on various aspects of Social upliftment and promotes macro, mezzo and micro aspects of professional social work practice.

Curriculum of Master of Social Work

The MSW online program curriculum is a blend of core and specialized courses in social work practices, policies, ethics and research.

Duration of Master of Social Work

An online MSW program takes two years to complete; although maximum it can be completed in 4 years.

Course Delivery

Master of Social Work is delivered through an online learning management system where Students can attend lectures, prepare assignments, and read course materials online. The online MSW program also offers live classes where students can interact with professors & classmates directly .

Flexibility

This Online program offers flexibility to study at their own pace and on their own schedules that will be beneficial for working professionals or individuals looking to advance their careers with other commitments.

Faculty Support

Students typically have access to faculty members and advisors through online communication channels, such as email or discussion forums, for academic guidance and support.

Field Practice

MSW programs require students to complete a field practice or internship, where they gain hands-on experience working with individuals, families, or communities in a supervised setting.

Admission Requirements

Admission to an online MSW program generally requires a bachelor’s degree from Recognized University or Equivalent.

Career Opportunities

Graduates of MSW programs can pursue careers in social work, including clinical social work, school social work, medical social work, mental health counseling, child welfare, substance abuse counseling, and more.
