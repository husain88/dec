Courses SMU Offers:-

The SMU distance learning program offers four courses: MBA, BBA, BCA, and MCA. Explore these courses in detail:

Bachelor’s of Business Administration (BBA)

SMU’s distance BBA program is a 3-year undergraduate degree program for students who want to pursue a career in the field of business and management. Students get access to these materials just after admission, after which they are independent because they do not need to attend the traditional classroom program. They can study their course at their own pace and at their convenience. The syllabus of these distance courses is updated according to the latest course needs and requirements. This BBA degree helps students become skilled and knowledgeable.

  • Eligibility: To pursue an MBA distance course, students need to complete their graduation in any field from a recognized university, either in a traditional classroom training program or through distance learning.
  • Scope: After completion of this course, you will have great job opportunities in the relevant field with a handsome salary package from the beginning of your career.
  • This course is divided into six semesters, and the fee for them is 16,250 INR.

Master’s of Business Administration (MBA)

SMU’s distance MBA program is a 2-year post graduation program, which is one of the most demanding courses to be done by students after completion of the graduation degree in any field. SMU provides pre-recorded lectures and other study materials to the students at their convenience so that they can study according to their time and preferences.

  • Eligibility: To pursue an MBA distance course, students need to complete their graduation in any field from a recognized university, either in a traditional classroom training program or through distance learning.
  • Scope: As it is said that the MBA degree is the most in-demand, every second student chooses to pursue this degree just because of the scope it provides after completing the course. After an MBA course, students are opened to a lot of opportunities, which provides them with better chances to enhance their careers and grow in their desired fields.
  • This course is divided into 4 semesters (two semesters a year), and the semester-wise fees are 21,250 INR.

The specialization course SMU offers is Marketing Management.

  • Information System Management
  • Human Resource Management
  • Financial Management

Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)

Students who pursue a BCA course are those who are interested in the fields of computer science and IT. BCA distance course helps students to guide them in the fields of advanced programming, coding languages, and other important computing fields. SMU provides the best of the pre-recorded lectures and a healthy studying environment to their distance education learners so that they don’t miss any important topics and get chances to enhance their capability, skills, and knowledge in their chosen field.

  • Eligibility: To pursue this particular course, students need to complete their 10+2 with mathematics and computer science as their optional subjects in their chosen stream.
  • Scope: After completing a BCA degree, students get a lot of job opportunities related to their specialization. SMU collaborates with the best business industries to provide placement after these courses. Students can anticipate a handsome salary package when they get placed through the college.
  • This course is a three-year degree program; hence, it is divided into six semesters, two each year, and the fees for each semester are 16,250 INR.

Master’s of Computer Application (MCA)

SMU provides an MCA distance course for students who seek a professional course in the field of computer science. Students get digital textbooks, pre-recorded lectures, and other study materials after enrolment. Students need to access their entire course, and they can re-read their lessons anytime, according to their time.

  • Eligibility: After completing a graduation degree in any field, they get access to enroll themselves in this distance learning program. However, BCA graduates are preferred first when it comes to counseling students.
  • Scope: After completing the distance MCA course from the SMU distance learning program, students are opened to a lot of job opportunities where they get a lot of exposure to the field of computer science and other related fields.
  • This course is a 2-year degree program that is divided into 4 semesters, and the fees per semester are 18,250 INR.

Concluding Remarks!

SMU distance learning programs are the best to pursue for students who are stuck in other professional commitments but want to continue their education to enhance their skills and be professionals in their respective fields.
