Online MBA Courses: The Key to Advancing Your Career

An online MBA program is a 2 year post graduate course in the dynamic business industry and can be a best degree program for individuals looking to uplift their career . However, attending classes on campus may only be feasible if you’re employed full-time or can relocate. Fortunately, the online Master of Business Administration online education programs offer a solution.

Enrolling in an online MBA program allows you to earn your degree from a respected university without stepping on campus. With the flexibility to study at your own pace and on your schedule, as long as you have an internet connection, you can pursue your education anywhere.

About Online MBA Programmes

MBA distance education is a correspondence postgraduate management program that can be completed in two years. It is a popular postgraduate degree program among students after graduation to work towards career growth in business management.The program provides flexibility to students as they can attend small MBA classes in a hybrid format and may need to visit the institute or centers during weekends. Online MBA program is a great career option for candidates who cannot pursue a traditional MBA program due to some reasons such as financial constraints, work schedules, belonging to rural areas, wanting to switch their career or others.

Online MBA courses are approved by UGC/AICTE and are offered by various universities and institutes in India. Several universities offering online MBA courses have established placement cells to help candidates secure positions in multinational companies, government jobs, and other roles such as Corporate Associate, Portfolio Manager, Finance Analyst, Credit Risk Manager, Executive, Senior Executive, etc.

The content and duration of the MBA online education program are equivalent to that of a full-time Master of Business Administration program, with a curriculum covering subjects such as Macroeconomics, Microeconomics, Operations Management, Corporate Finance, and other related disciplines. Despite differences in study style and access to campus resources, the syllabus of a Distance MBA is similar to that of full-time MBA programs.

While the different types of distance MBA courses available through online education make it a highly sought-after management degree, it shouldn’t be the only factor considered when deciding whether to pursue it. Online MBA courses provide students with professional and leadership skills, for example how to manage or organize a business? What strategies can be used to start a new venture etc.?

Why to Pursue an Online MBA program?

Lower Cost

Another advantage of an online MBA distance education is the lower cost compared to on-campus programs. You won’t have to worry about the costs of relocating or commuting to campus, and some online programs may offer tuition discounts.

Flexibility

Online MBA courses give an opportunity to students across the nation to pursue their course as there is no restricted schedule of learning or joining physical classes. The student can take classes, complete assignments, attempt quizzes and scheduled examinations as per their convenience. Students who cannot afford costly regular courses can opt for a distance learning programme as it is comparatively less hefty and suits their pocket. The subjects taught in online MBA are almost the same as in regular MBA and enhance the managerial skills required in the actual functioning of the industry.

Global Reach

Online MBA education programs are accessible from anywhere in the world, which means you can enroll in a program offered by a prestigious university without leaving your home country.

Curriculum

Online Master of Business Administration education programs offer a curriculum similar to traditional on-campus MBA programs. You are required to study core subjects along with the specialized subjects in management that develop the individual skills needed to succeed in a business world.

Admission Requirements

Admission requirements for online MBA distance education programs are similar to those of on-campus Master of Business Administration programs. Most programs require a bachelor’s degree from an accredited institution, a minimum GPA, and sometimes work experience.

Course Delivery

Online MBA courses are typically delivered through video lectures, online discussions, and assignments while some programs may require you to attend virtual classes or participate in group projects.

Accreditation

Accreditation is an essential factor to consider when choosing an online MBA distance education program. Accreditation plays a crucial role in ensuring the­ quality and standards of online distance education programs offere­d by universities and institutions in India.

Career Advancement

A Master of Business Administration degree offers career assistance to their learners for better future prospects. With an online MBA program,Various internship programs, webinars, and training are organized from time to time by the University to boost the morale and personality of the students.

Who Should Pursue Online MBA?

The MBA online education program is an ideal choice for those:

  • Professionals who wish to pursue their higher studies while working
  • Candidates who cannot afford the high cost of the regular MBA course.
  • Students who did not score good in CAT/MAT/GMAT entrance exams and failed to get enrolled in on-campus MBA programs.
  • Students from rural areas who are unable to attend regular on-campus classes due to geographical issues.
  • Individuals from any stream who want to switch their career to the business management line.

The online program offers the flexibility to attend classes from anywhere and anytime as per their convenience and can be completed in the same duration as a regular MBA program. With the advancement of technology, students can access digital books and attend pre-recorded lectures through distance mode.

Online MBA Eligibility Criteria

To be admitted into an online MBA course, applicants must meet the specific admission requirements of each university or college. The following eligibility criteria must be met by students:

  • Candidates must have completed a bachelor’s degree from an accredited university with a minimum of 50 per cent marks. Any graduate candidate is eligible to apply for the Distance MBA program.
  • There is no age limit for distance MBA eligibility. However, candidates must have passed the colleges’ certified MBA admission exams before admission.

Online MBA Entrance Exams 2024

While some institutes require students to appear for an entrance exam to enroll in their distance MBA course, others have different admission procedures.

  • Symbiosis Centre for Distance Learning (SCDL) considers scores of management exams such as CAT, XAT, MAT, and ATMA, among others.
  • Conversely, NMIMS Global Access School for Continuing Education admits students based on eligibility criteria, which include a Bachelor’s degree from any recognised university with 50 per cent aggregate marks and two years of work experience.
  • At IGNOU, admission to the distance Master of Business Administration program is based on the performance of the candidates in IGNOU OPENMAT, which is conducted twice a year.

Distance MBA Online Courses

Upon completing the distance MBA courses, candidates are awarded a postgraduate degree or diploma, depending on the institute they enroll in.

  • Institutes like Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies (NMIMS), Global Access School for Continuing Education (NGA-SCE), IMT, and Symbiosis Centre for Distance Learning offer Post Graduate Diplomas (PGDs).
  • In contrast, Sikkim Manipal University, Amity University, Manipal University Jaipur, LPU and many more offer MBA degree programs.
  • IGNOU offers online MBA courses, Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM), and PGD degrees, while the Welingkar Institute of Management offers a PGDM degree.

Online MBA Course Subjects and Syllabus

The online MBA course is divided into four semesters, where the curriculum includes core and elective subjects that vary based on the chosen specialization. The first two semesters primarily cover core subjects, while the remaining two semesters focus on elective subjects or specialization.

These are some of the common subjects and distance MBA course that students would study in the first year (semester I and II): Principles and Practice of Management, Management Economics, Organizational Behavior, Business Law, Management Accounting, Production/Operation Management, Financial Management, Human Resource Management, Marketing Management, Management Information system.

The specialization subjects offered in the second year of a distance MBA course can vary depending on the university or institution offering the program. However, some common specializations in MBA programs include Finance, Marketing, Human Resources, Operations Management, Information Technology, and International Business.

Online MBA Course Duration

The MBA course typically lasts for a minimum of 2 years and a maximum of 4 years, with an additional 2-year period for completing all exams. The curriculum is divided into four semesters of six months each.

Process of Admission

To apply for admission to a distance MBA course, you can follow these general steps:

  1. Visit the official website of the university where you want to apply.
  2. Register as a new user and complete the application form with your personal information.
  3. Submit the required documents and the application form and pay the registration fee as mentioned on the website.
  4. Choose a mode of payment, which may include net banking, UPI payment, or demand draft.
  5. Once the payment is complete, you will receive a confirmation email with your student enrollment or number.
