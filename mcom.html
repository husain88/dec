Online M.Com Eligibility Criteria

Candidates who wish to pursue an online M.Com course in India must meet the following requirements:

  • The applicants must have a bachelor’s degree from a recognised institution.
  • The candidate must have an overall GPA of between 45% and 50%.
  • Candidates must possess the necessary skills to enroll in an M.Com course online (a background in math or business is advantageous).

Online M.Com Course Duration

The M.Com online course has a two-year duration with a curriculum that is separated into four semesters. The online­ program offers a flexible time­frame, with a maximum duration of 4 years. This makes it suitable­ for working professionals who need to balance­ their studies with other commitme­nts.

Online M.Com Admission Procedure

  • To begin the registration procedure and submit an application for the course, go to the enquiry form at CDEC official website.
  • Use your email address and phone number to create a new user account.
  • Give your information on the application form.
  • Digital copies of the required documents must be submitted via the website for university verification.
  • The academic fee must then be paid via one of the online payment methods (Net Banking, UPI Payment, Demand Draft, etc.).
  • After your application has been examined and accepted, you will receive a confirmation email from the institution.
