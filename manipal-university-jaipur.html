Manipal University Jaipur is a state private university launched in the year 2011, on an invitation from the Government of Rajasthan. Manipal University Jaipur (MUJ) has an A+ NAAC ranking with a cumulative grade point average (CGPA) of 3.28

MUJ offers online degree courses all over India and abroad. This means that Online Manipal provides flexible learning opportunities, e-learning material, and full-fledged programme delivery through the internet using technology assisted mechanisms and resources.

Manipal University Jaipur offers career-oriented courses at Undergraduate & Postgraduate Levels including management, commerce, computer applications, and journalism as well.

Online courses offered by Manipal University are BBA, BCA, B.Com, MBA, M.Com, MCA, and MA in journalism and mass communication.

In fields such as data science, management, information technology, commerce, and others, the online Manipal University offers bachelor’s and master’s level courses delivered through an LMS with live faculty interaction.

Under Graduate Programmes offered by the University

Online BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration)

BBA is a three-year undergraduate course consisting of six semesters offering adequate business-related knowledge and skills to the students.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 from a recognized state or national board
  • 10+3 year diploma from a recognized state or national institute
  • Students must have at least 45% marks in their 10+2 / diploma (40% is for reserved categories)

Fees Structure

  • INR 22,500 (each semester)
  • Total fees: INR 1,35,000 (all-inclusive)
Online B.Com (Bachelor of Commerce)

B.COM is a three-year undergraduate course that is divided into six-equal semesters focused on concepts of financial accounting, taxation, business laws, and others.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 from a recognized state or national board
  • 10+3 year diploma from a recognized state or national institute
  • Students must have at least 50% marks in their 10+2 / diploma (45% is for reserved categories)

Fees Structure

  • INR 16,500 (each semester)
  • Total fees: INR 99,000 (all-inclusive)
Online BCA (Bachelor in Computer Applications)

BCA is a three-year undergraduate course consisting of six semesters imparting computer applications, software, computing language, and so on.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 from a recognized state or national board
  • 10+3 year diploma from a recognized state or national institute
  • Students must have at least 45% marks in their 10+2 / diploma (40% is for reserved categories)

Fees Structure

  • INR 22,500 (each semester)
  • Total fees: INR 1,35,000 (all-inclusive)

Post Graduate Programmes offered by the University

Online MBA (Master of Business Administration)

MBA is a two-year degree program that is divided into 4 semesters with 13 career-focused specializations. Manipal online MBA program along with its specialization creates successful career paths for their learners and make them stand out as a leader in today’s competitive world.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Students must have 10+2+3 year bachelor’s degree from a recognized university/ institution or equivalent qualification by AIU approval
  • Student must have a minimum of 50% of aggregate marks in their graduation (45% is for reserved categories)
  • Candidates need to crack the online aptitude test that is conducted by Manipal University Jaipur
  • Candidates need to score a valid (40 percentile) in their aptitude test (CAT/MAT/CMAT/XAT/GMAT)

Elective Specialization

  • Finance
  • Marketing
  • HRM
  • Analytics & Data Science
  • BFSI
  • IT & FinTech
  • Operations Management
  • International Business
  • Information System Management
  • Project Management
  • Supply chain Management
  • Total Quality Management

Fees Structure

  • INR 43,750 (each semester)
  • Total fees: INR 1,75,000 (all-inclusive)
Online MCA (Master of Computer Application)

MCA is a two-year post graduate degree program consisting of 4 semesters. The course is mainly concerned with Information Technology & hence gives a deeper knowledge of computer systems, operating systems, computing language, system development, networking, software development, software engineering, troubleshooting, etc.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Students must have a 10+2+3 year bachelor’s degree from a recognised university/ institution or equivalent qualification in computer applications/computer science/informational technology, recognized by AIU & other competent bodies.
  • Students must have a minimum of 50% of aggregate marks in their graduation (45% is for reserved categories)
  • Candidates who belong from streams like science, arts & humanities, business administration, business management, commerce, etc. students must have a 10+2+3 year bachelor’s degree from a recognized university, with mathematics at the 10+2 level with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks in graduation (45% reserved categories)

Fees Structure

  • INR 39,500 (each semester)
  • Total fees: INR 1,58,000 (all-inclusive)
Online M.Com (Master of Commerce)

M.com is a two-year postgraduate degree course that deals with the areas of commerce, accounting, management, and economics. If you are looking to make a career in banking, finance, accounting and different commerce sectors, this course is best-suited for you.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Students must have 10+2+3 year bachelor’s degree from a recognized university/institution or equivalent degree recognized by AIU
  • Students must have a minimum of 50% aggregate marks in their graduation (45% is reserved categories)

Fees Structure

  • INR 27,000 (each semester)
  • Total fees: INR 1,08,000 (all-inclusive)
Online MA JMC (Master of Arts in Journalism & Mass Communication)

MAJMC is a 2-year postgraduate degree programme in the field of mass communication and journalism. If you are looking for a career in journalism, mass communiqué and media, this online course is for you.

Eligibility Criteria

  • university/institution or equivalent qualification recognized by AIU

Manipal University Jaipur is recognized by the UGC (University Grant Commission), AICTE, WES, ICAS and accredited A+ by NAAC (National Assessment and Accreditation Council).

  • Step 2: Fill all the education details
  • Step 3: payment of course fee
  • Step 4: Upload the Documents
  • Step 5: The University will then send you a confirmation mail along with the enrollment ID.
