D.Y Patil University Online Programs

Dr. D. Y. Patil Vidyapeeth, Pune, was accorded the status of Deemed to be University in 2003. It is one of the largest online learning education universities in India providing quality education to students. The University has 34 years of excellence in education & is ranked at 41st position in University Category India by NIRF & has received A++ grade from the NAAC. The other major accreditations and approvals of DPU Online University have been listed below:

  • UGC-DEB (University Grants Commission-Distance Education Board),
  • AICTE (All India Council of Technical Education)
  • AIU (Association of Indian Universities)
  • ACU (Association of Commonwealth Universities), the ISO (International Standards Organization)
  • WES (World Education Services)

Under Graduate Programmes offered by the University

DY Patil University online courses equip learners with skills and knowledge that enable them to join the job market confidently, continue higher education or travel across borders. 


Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

BBA is a full-time online graduation course for three years. It’s a professional degree program for students looking to start a career in management & business administration.

Specializations

The specializations offered at DPU-COL’s online BBA Programme include:

  • Marketing Management
  • Logistics and Supply chain management
  • Event Management
  • Human Resource Management
  • International Business Management
  • Hospital Management
  • Retail Operations

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 or an equivalent qualification with English as one of the languages from a recognized board

Fees Structure

  • INR 18000/- per semester
  • Total Course Fee: INR 1,08,000/- (It excludes 1500/- admission fee one time)

Post Graduate Programmes offered by the University

Master of Business Administration (MBA)

MBA is a full-time online post-graduation course for two years. After completing Online MBA from DPU the candidates will gain the right skills, knowledge, and attitude required to become successful in the global business environment.

Specialization

The specializations offered at DPU-COL’s online MBA Programme include:

  • Sales & Marketing
  • Human Resource Management
  • Finance Management
  • Retail Management
  • Entrepreneurship Management
  • Logistics & Supply Chain Management
  • Hospital and Healthcare Management
  • International Business Management

Eligibility Criteria

  • A candidate applying for programme should have passed a Bachelor’s degree from a recognized university in any discipline.
  • Minimum 40% aggregate marks or 45% in case of candidates belonging to reserved categories

Fees Structure

  • INR 41,800/- per semester
  • Total Course Fee: INR 1,67,200/- (It excludes 1500/- admission fee one time)
