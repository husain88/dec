Shiv Nadar University Online Programs

Shiv Nadar University, Delhi NCR (SNU) was established in 2011 as a multidisciplinary, student-centric and research-focused university. The University was presented as a “Gift to the Nation”, accepted by the President of India in 2016. The University has been recognized as an Institution of Eminence by the Ministry of Education, Government of India, one of the four private institutions in India at present. The Shiv Nadar aims to be an internationally recognized University for research and creative ventures.

Master of Business Administration (MBA)

MBA is a 2-year online program that is covered over 8 quarters designed for candidates who are interested in up skilling their business management knowledge. Irrespective of other commitments, students can prepare themselves to be professionally fit in an increasingly volatile, uncertain, and digital world.

Specializations

Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicants must hold a bachelor’s degree or equivalent with at least 50% marks or equivalent CGPA from any of the recognized Universities, Deemed Universities and Institutions of National Importance or from a Professional Body including ICAI, ICSI, ICWAI, etc.
  • Graduates with professional working experience will be preferred
