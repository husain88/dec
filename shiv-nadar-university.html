Shiv Nadar University Online Programs

Shiv Nadar University, Delhi NCR (SNU) was established in 2011 as a multidisciplinary, student-centric and research-focused university. The University was presented as a “Gift to the Nation”, accepted by the President of India in 2016. The University’s goal is to become internationally recognized for the quality of its research and creative endeavors and their applicability to improving quality of life, generating new insights and expanding knowledge and creativity.
The University has been recognized as an Institution of Eminence by the Ministry of Education, Government of India. It is one of the four private institutions in India at present to have received this prestigious recognition, enabling it to emerge as a world-class teaching and research institution.

University is offering Only MBA degree program in Online Mode. The details of the course is mentioned below

Master of Business Administration (MBA)

MBA is a 2-year, online program that is covered over 8 quarters. The Program is designed for fresh graduates and working professionals to provide them an effective business education in an online platform. It prepares participants to lead in an increasingly volatile, uncertain, complex, ambiguous, and digital world.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicants must hold a bachelor’s degree or equivalent with at least 50% marks or equivalent CGPA from any of the recognized Universities, Deemed Universities, Institutions of National Importance or from a Professional Body including ICAI, ICSI, ICWAI, etc.
  • Graduates with professional working experience will be preferred

Specialization offered in MBA

  • DATA SCIENCE & ANALYTICS
  • HUMAN RESOURCES
  • MARKETING
  • DIGITAL FINANCE
