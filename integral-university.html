University Accreditations & Recognitions

Integral University Online Programs

Integral University is a premier educational institution located in Lucknow. Integral University was established in 2004. The University is approved by the University Grants Commission under Sections 2(f) and 12B of the UGC Act, 1956, MCI, PCI, BCI, INC, CoA, NCTE, UPSMF, IAP & DEB. Member of AIU & is accredited by the NAAC to offer Open & Distance Learning to students. Integral University has a unique culture of inclusiveness, diversity, personal and intellectual integrity and value-based education.

Why to Choose Online Integral University India?

Affordable fees structure

Integral University Online has designed the affordable and pocket-friendly fee structure for their learners so that every student can pursue studies which they are unable to do due to high fee structures and lack of facilities.

No restricted schedules of learning

The online programs of Integral University gives an opportunity to students across the nation to pursue their course as there is no restricted schedule of learning or joining physical classes. The student can take classes, complete assignments, attempt quizzes as per their convenience.

Easy paper pattern

The examination pattern of Integral University Online is the easiest opted pattern which includes internal and term end examinations. The internal assessment has a weightage of 20-30% and they are completed by students before examination whereas term end examinations have a weightage of 70-80% which comprises MCQ and subjective questions.

Recognized Degree

An online degree awarded by Integral University holds similar recognition as that of a regular degree. Thus this online degree is accepted by the top recruiters globally.

Career Assistance

Integral University Online offers career assistance to its learners for better future prospects. Various internship programs, webinars, and training are organized from time to time by the University to boost the morale and personality of the students.
