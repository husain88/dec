10 Best Online MBA Colleges in India 2023

The demand for online MBA programs has been growing steadily, espec­ially among indiv­iduals who are already working profes­sionals or those looking to earn a profes­sional degree without the const­raints of attending a physical college or unive­rsity. Online MBA programs India offer a convenient option for people who are employed or live in areas without access to reputable business schools. They allow individuals to pursue their degrees from renowned universities without leaving their homes or offices.

Due to advancements in online technology, top business schools such as Amity University, GLA University, Shiv Nadar University, IGNOU, NMIMS, and others have embraced virtual classrooms as their primary teaching method. As a result, almost every well-known business school in India now offers an online MBA program. These online MBA courses are accredited by the University Grants Commission and the Distance Education Bureau, ensuring their legitimacy and quality.

1. Amity University

Amity University Online is dedicated to establishing a transformative learning environment that embraces digital classrooms, providing education accessibility and mobility from any location. It upholds Amity’s vision of nation-building through education. Recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC), Amity University Online is India’s first university to offer online degree, diploma, and certification programs.

Amity Unive­rsity is renowned for its best online MBA program, offering a diverse selection of speciali­zations, including Entrepre­neurship & Leade­rship, Finance & Accou­nting, Global Finance Market, Hospi­tality Manag­ement, Human Resource Manag­ement, Infor­mation Techn­ology, Intern­ational Business, Marketing & Sales, O­perations Managemen­t, Retail Manageme­nt, Data Science, Digital Marke­ting.

Eligibility Criteria
  • Candidates must have obtained a diploma for admission to online MBA courses.
  • The applicant must have a sufficient command of the English language.
Fees Structure
  • Total Course fee: INR 2,53,000/-
  • Semester Fee: Rs. 63,250/-
2. Manipal University

With an impressive A+ NAAC ranking and a cumulative grade point average (CGPA) of 3.28, Manipal University Jaipur (MUJ) stands as a reputable institution. Online Manipal offers flexible learning options, providing e-learning materials and comprehensive program delivery through technology-assisted mechanisms and resources. The online MBA course offered by Manipal University is carefully crafted to equip students with essential management, administration, finance, and marketing skills, enabling them to enhance their professional capabilities. It comes in the list of the top online MBA programs in India.

Eligibility Criteria
  • Students must possess a 10+2+3-year bachelor’s degree from an accredited university or other institution or an equivalent credential as determined by the AIU.
  • Students must graduate with at least 50% of their total marks (45% for protected categories).
  • Candidates must succeed in the Manipal University Jaipur’s online aptitude test.
  • The minimum passing result for candidates’ aptitude tests (CAT/MAT/CMAT/XAT/GMAT) is valid (40 percentile).
Fees Structure
  • Total Course fee: INR 1,50,000/-
3. Dr. D. Y. Patil University

With a remarkable 34-year legacy of delivering quality education, the University has earned recognition in the education sector. According to NIRF rankings, it holds the 41st position in the University Category in India and has achieved an outstanding A++ grade from the NAAC. Earning an Online MBA from DPU equips candi­dates with the necessary skills, knowl­edge, and mindset to excel in today’s compe­titive global business lands­cape. This program sets indiv­iduals on a traje­ctory towards success.

Eligibility Criteria
  • Any applicant for the programme must have earned their Bachelor’s degree from an accredited institution to be admitted into the best online MBA.
  • Minimum of 50% overall, or 45% for candidates who fall under a reserved category.
Fees Structure
  • Total Course fee: INR 1,30,000/-
4. Chandigarh University

Established in 2012, Chandigarh University is one of Punjab’s premier universities. With UGC-DEB approval and an esteemed A+ Grade accreditation from NAAC, CU is known for its excellence in education. The university provides:

  • Exceptional learning opportunities.
  • Allowing students to benefit from the expertise of renowned university professionals.
  • Ensuring a top-notch educational experience while pursuing the best online MBA.

Eligibility Criteria
  • A Bachelor’s degree with at least 50% of the marks required in any discipline from an accredited university or institution.
Fees Structure
  • Total Course fee: INR 2,00,000/-
  • Per Semester fee: INR 50,000/-
5. Lovely Professional University (LPU)

Lovely Professional University (LPU) is a renowned private university in Jalandhar, Punjab, India. Founded in 2005, LPU has rapidly earned a reput­ation for its excep­tional acade­mics, cuttin­g-edge teaching techn­iques, and advanced facil­ities. The university has received accreditation from esteemed organizations such as the University Grants Commission (UGC), All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), the National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE), and the Association of Indian Universities (AIU). LPU University offers an esteemed online MBA Course.

Eligibility Criteria
  • To be eligible for this program, candidates must have completed a Bachelor’s degree in any discipline from a recognized University or Institution.
Fees Structure
  • Total Course fee: INR 1,56,000/-
  • Semester fee: INR 39,00/-
6. Jain University

Jain University, established in 1990 and situated in the bustling Silicon Valley of India, Bengaluru, is a prestigious Deemed-to-be university. Recognized by UGC and AICTE, the university has achieved an impressive A++ ranking from NAAC. Furthermore, it has received certifications from IMA and CIM, further enhancing its reputation. Jain Unive­rsity provides a variety of elective courses for their online MBA program. Students can choose from special­izations such as Finance and Marke­ting, Intern­ational Finance, General Manag­ement, Human Resource Manag­ement, Infor­mation Techn­ology Manag­ement, Logis­tics, and Supply Chain Manag­ement. These options allow students to pursue their indiv­idual interests and align with their career goals.

Eligibility Criteria
  • Pass in any stream of an undergraduate (Bachelor’s) program from a UGC-recognized university after completing at least three (3) years of study with a minimum cumulative grade point average of 50% or an equivalent. SC/ST applicants will receive a 5% relaxation.
  • Candidates in the bachelor’s program’s final semester are also welcome to apply.
Fees Structure
  • Total Course fee: INR 1,20,000/-
  • Sem fee: INR 30,000/-
7. IGNOU

Initially focused on open/distance mode courses, the university has progressed to offer online programs to cater to its learners. This esteemed online university in India holds accreditation and recognition from various reputable bodies such as UGC, UGC-DEB, NCTE, AICTE, INC, and NAAC (A++). The online MBA program India offered by this university is a post-graduate degree program specifically designed for both freshers and working professionals, equipping them with the necessary skills for successful careers in business and management. Students have the oppor­tunity to speci­alize in various fields such as Marketing Manag­ement, Human Resource Manag­ement, Financial Manag­ement, Services Manag­ement, and more.

Eligibility Criteria
  • Students must have earned a Bachelor’s degree in any field from an accredited university.
  • Minimum of 50% overall, or 45% for candidates that fall under the category of “reserved,”
Fees Structure
  • Total Course fee: INR 58,000/-
8. Mizoram University

MZU, a university approved by UGC-DEB, holds the notable position of 78th among the top universities in India according to the NIRF Ranking. The university has received a commendable A-grade accreditation from NAAC. In their online MBA course, MZU provides various elective special­izations to choose from. These include Marke­ting, Finance, Entrepre­neurship, Big Data Analy­tics, and more. These diverse specializations cater to the specific interests and career aspirations of students.

Eligibility Criteria
  • All PG programs will grant applicants with relevant work experience of at least two years a waiver from the requirements for graduation or the equivalent through the 10 + 2 + 3 system.
Fees Structure
  • Total Course Fee: INR 43,200/-
  • Fee Year Wise: INR 21,500/-
9. Shiv Nadar University

Shiv Nadar University has earned the prestigious recognition as an Institution of Eminence by the Ministry of Education, Government of India.

The unive­rsity strives for global recog­nition through outst­anding research and creative endea­vors. Its main goal is to improve the quality of life, gain new insights, and foster knowledge and creat­ivity.

Shiv Nadar Univer­sity’s MBA program is designed for both recent graduates and profes­sionals currently working. This program provides a thorough education in business through an online platform. Partic­ipants gain the essential skills and knowledge to thrive in today’s dynamic world, marked by constant change and digital advanc­ements, and charac­terized by volat­ility, uncert­ainty, compl­exity, and ambig­uity.

Eligibility Criteria
  • Candidates must possess a bachelor’s degree or its equivalent from one of the recognized universities, deemed universities, institutions of national importance, or from a professional body such as ICAI, ICSI, ICWAI, etc., with at least 50% of the total possible marks or an equivalent grade point average.
  • It will be preferred if graduates have professional work experience.
Fees Structure
  • Total Course fee: INR 4,00,000/-
10. GLA University

GLA Online University has obtained approvals from esteemed bodies, including UGC, AICTE, BCI, and others, solidifying its credibility. Today, GLA University has established itself as a prestigious institution of higher education in North India. Additionally, GLA Online has garnered recognition from the Government of Uttar Pradesh.

The online MBA course offered by GLA Online University equips professionals with specialized knowledge and a diverse skill set encompassing critical analysis, presentation skills, public speaking, interpersonal communication, leadership, attitude, and multitasking abilities. These comprehensive offerings prepare aspiring individuals to thrive as organizational managers in today’s dynamic global business landscape.

Eligibility Criteria
  • Candidate must have completed a Graduation Level Course from a Recognised University (At Least 3 Years)
  • Candidates from All Fields Are Welcome to Apply
  • Candidate must have received at least 50% of the possible points at graduation.
  • Candidates from SC, ST, OBC, or any other Reserved Category must have received at least 45% of their grade point average in such categories.
Fees Structure
  • Total Course fee: INR 1,74,000/-
  • Sem fee: INR 24,000/-
