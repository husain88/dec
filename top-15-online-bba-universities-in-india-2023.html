Top 15 Online BBA Universities in India 2023

Business adminis­tration has become increa­singly popular, and earning a Bachelor of Business Adminis­tration (BBA) degree can lead to diverse career paths. With advanc­ements in techn­ology, online education offers a flexible and conve­nient option for indiv­iduals seeking higher educa­tion.

An online BBA in open university qualifies an individual for marketing positions. With a BBA, candidates are not only open to employment with large organisations but also have the chance to pursue their dreams of starting their own businesses.

An online BBA course in India is a smart decision to start a successful financial future because it helps students understand market concepts and spot potential business opportunities. For further information on BBA online learning, continue reading.

1. Amity University

Amity University was founded in 2005 as a result of the Uttar Pradesh Act 2005 (Act 11 of 2005), an act of the State Legislature. It is one of the top private online universities accredited by the University Grant Commission.

As a continuation of Amity’s mission to strengthen the nation through education, Amity University Online is committed to developing a transformative learning environment, a digital classroom with true mobility and access to education from anywhere. The first institution in India to be approved by the UGC to provide online BBA degrees, diplomas, and certification programmes is Amity Institution Online.

Amity University Online offers a comprehensive BBA course online that combines theoretical knowledge with practical application. The curriculum covers core business subjects, including accounting, finance, marketing, and operations management.

Eligibility Criteria
  • 10th Class Certificate (Completing 10 years of Formal Schooling)
  • 12th Class Certificate (Completing 12 years of Formal Schooling)
Fees Structure
  • Total Course fee: INR 1,50,000/-
  • Semester Fee: Rs. 25,000/-
2. Manipal University Jaipur

Being a state-owned private institution, Manipal Institution was established in 2011 at the government of Rajasthan’s invitation. NAAC had accredited the Manipal University Jaipur (MUJ) with an A+ rank and has a cumulative grade point average (CGPA) of 3.28.

In India and overseas, MUJ offers online education. This indicates that Online Manipal uses technology-assisted mechanisms and resources to give complete programmes, flexible learning possibilities, and e-learning content over the Internet.

Manipal University is acknowledged and accredited by the University Grants Commission (UGC), The Association of Commonwealth Universities (ACU), NAAC, All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), WES (World Education Services), and many other organisations.

At the undergraduate and graduate levels, Manipal University provides career-focused BBA courses online in management, commerce, computer applications, and journalism.

Check out C-DEC (Center for Distance Education Courses) for BBA admission online.

Eligibility Criteria
  • Students must have passed the 10+2 or any equivalent examination from a recognized institution with a minimum 50% aggregate for pursuing the online BBA in open university.
Fees Structure
  • Total Course fee: INR 1,26,000/-
  • Semester Fee: Rs. 21,000/-
3. Jain University

A deemed university founded in 1990 and situated in Bengaluru, India’s Silicon Valley, is the Jain University. AICTE and UGC both acknowledge the online BBA in open university. It received a NAAC accreditation of A++.

Students can choose electives from a variety of fields for their online BBA degree, including finance, marketing, human resources management, international marketing, multimedia management, logistics and supply chain management, engineering, and project management.

Eligibility Criteria
  • Successfully complete the 10+2 examination from a State Board, CBSE, NIOS, IGCSE, IB, or ICSE that is approved by the State or Central Government.
  • Candidates scheduled to take the 10+2 test are also welcome to apply to the best online BBA university.
Fees Structure
  • Total Course fee: INR 1,76,500/-
  • Fee Per Sem: INR 27,500/-
4. KL University

KL university was established in 1980-81 as KL College of Engineering and attained autonomous status in 2006. It was conferred with Deemed University status in the year 2009, under section 3 of the UGC act 1956.

KL University has been granted Category 1 Status, accorded with A++ Grading earlier by NAAC, and a score of 3.57/4 by UGC. It ranks in the list of the top 10 universities for online BBA in India in the country. In 2019, UGC and MHRD declared this institution as a Category 1 institution.

The university’s online BBA degree is designed to develop managerial skills and business acumen. The curri­culum places emphasis on key areas such as business communi­cation, finance, econo­mics, organiz­ational behavior, and strategic manag­ement.

Eligibility Criteria
  • Passed the 10+2 or equivalent examination from any recognized Board with a minimum grade point average of 50%.
Fees Structure
  • Total Course fee: INR 67,500/- including Admission Fee of INR 1000/-
  • Per Sem fee: INR 10,000/-

Job Opportunities After Online BBA

BBA provides a valuable oppor­tunity for students who are undecided about their future career path and want to explore multiple options. By studying BBA, students can develop essential skills in areas like marke­ting, sales, finance, opera­tions, HR, and even IT. These areas along with management and leadership skills allow one to get job opportunities in the Marketing, Sales, Finance, and Human Resource sectors in almost all industries. A student can expect a package of around 4 to 10 LPA after completing his/her online BBA program.
