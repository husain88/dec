About Distance Education Program

The Distance MBA program, a two-year management study course or MBA degree correspondence course in India, is perfect for those who can’t make it to regular classes – and that’s why students love it. The study plan and length of a MBA distance course are pretty much like those of full-time MBAs, but the way you learn and the campus perks aren’t quite the same. In your distance MBA course, you’ll dive into big picture economics and the nitty-gritty of running a business, not to mention mastering corporate finance and other money matters. The instructional delivery for these courses adopts a hybrid format, with the possibility of weekend visits to the institute or designated centers.
