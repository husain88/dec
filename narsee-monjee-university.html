Under Graduate Programmes offered by the University

Bachelor of commerce (B.com)

Eligibility Criteria

  • HSC (10+2) in any discipline from a recognized board with minimum 50%
  • HSC (10+2) in any discipline from a recognized board with 45% and minimum 2 years work experience
  • SSC (10) + 3 years Diploma recognized by AICTE with 55%

Duration

  • 3 years (6 semesters)

Fees Structure

  • 18000/- per semester
  • Total: 1,08,000/-
  • In addition, Students need to pay an examination fee of Rs 600/- per subject.
Bachelor in Business Administration (BBA)

Eligibility Criteria

  • HSC (10+2) in any discipline from a recognized board with minimum 50%
  • HSC (10+2) in any discipline from a recognized board with 45% and minimum 2 years work experience
  • SSC (10) + 3 years Diploma recognized by AICTE with 55%

Duration

  • 3 years (6 semesters)

Specialization

  • General
  • Business Analytics

Fees Structure

  • 25000/- per semester (in case of General)
  • 25000/- per semester (in case of Business Analytics) for 1st, 2nd & 30000/- per semester for 3rd onwards
  • Total fee: 1,70,000/-
  • In addition, Students need to pay an examination fee of Rs 600/- per subject.
