University Accreditations & Recognitions

Indira Gandhi National Open University

The Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU), established by an Act of Parliament in 1985. It is the largest Open University in the world & it has its main center in New Delhi.

Initially the university is offering courses in open/distance mode; it has now upgraded to provide online courses to their learners. This online University in India is accredited and has been recognized by UGC, UGC-DEB, NCTE, AICTE, INC, NAAC (A++) etc.

IGNOU offers many UG, PG, Diploma & Certificate courses like BA, BSW, BCA, MCA, M. COM, M.Sc., MBA Event Management, Environmental Studies, Information Security, Performing Arts, Tourism Studies, etc. IGNOU aims to reach students in all corners of the globe through its web-based study material, video lectures and live counseling.
