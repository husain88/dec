What is an Online BA Course?

A Bachelor of Arts (BA) degree can be obtained online in two different ways: as a BA general degree or as a BA Honors degree. Students who pursue a BA general degree receive an overview of all the major courses. With a BA Honors degree, students are able to focus on a single subject and gain a deeper understanding of it.

BA online degree is a Bachelor’s degree programme after 10+2. Online BA program is UGC approved course in India that provide the students with the in-depth knowledge of fine arts, encouraging them to focus in areas of their interest such as History, Political Science, Literature, Philosophy, etc.

Universities offering online BA degrees provide a learning platform for individuals and working professionals to pursue their higher education without having to attend the lectures physically. This is made possible through a flexible­ and remote learning structure­. Various UG, PG, and diploma courses are offered by numerous renowned top colleges.

The majority of BA online courses use online platforms to deliver interactive lectures, homework assignments, discussion boards, and exams. enabling the students to access a wide range of comprehensive study material while participating in various group discussions and learning activities with the help of online learning platforms.

A student can choose a wide range of disciplines to study for their BA online degree. A student can pursue an online BA in India in various main areas, including history, political science, literature, philosophy, foreign languages, etc., according to their area of interest. So, if you graduated from high school in any stream and are interested in a particular field, you can pursue an online BA degree course.

Eligibility for Online BA Course

A government-recognized board’s 10+2 graduation requirement, or an equivalent, with at least 45–50% of the possible marks, is required for online BA courses in India. However, the minimal qualifying requirements vary depending on the college selected.

What is the Online BA Duration?

The typical length of an online BA course is 3 years minimum and 6 years maximum. There are 6 semesters in the curriculum, with 2 semesters per year.
