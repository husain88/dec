Galgotias University

Galgotias University is a private university located in Uttar Pradesh, India established by the state of Uttar Pradesh under section 2(f) of the UGC Act, 1956; the university commenced its operations in 2011-12.
Through its online platform, Galgotias University Online offers a diverse selection of programs across various disciplines, including business administration, commerce, information technology and more. These programs are designed to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and practical experience required to succeed in their chosen fields.
The university’s online programs also provide flexibility in terms of scheduling, allowing students to balance their education with other commitments. This flexibility makes Galgotias University Online an ideal choice for working professionals, individuals seeking career advancement, and those who prefer the convenience of online learning.

Under Graduate Programmes offered by the University

Bachelor of Business Administration

The online BBA program is a three-year program provides a strong foundation in core business concepts, such as accounting, marketing, operations management, and business communication.
