Explore Everything About SMU Distance Learning

Sikkim Manipal University is a UGC DEC-approved distance university for students who seek distance education due to their busy schedules or any other reason. SMU offers a diverse range of distance courses and they are designed with the new technologies. After completion of the course students get an opportunity to get the best job offers.

If you check out the website of Sikkim Manipal University, you will find out that they offer undergraduate and postgraduate programs with a wide range of specializations and expert professionals and scholars to guide students throughout the course.

This university is approved by the Indian Education Council and board ministries of education such as the Medical Council of India, Nursing Institutions of India, and AICTE. This blog will highlight the SMU distance learning programs, their features, and other parameters.

Key Features of SMU Distance Courses

When you talk about distance education, it comes with the question of how they work and what the features of the university you are choosing to pursue your distance course are

Digital Study Material

Since SMU offers up-to-date, digital, and relevant content to their students in their distance courses, discussing remote education may be a suitable option for students. They have modified their curriculum to meet the most recent academic standards.

They make sure that their syllabus covers every topic that is pertinent to that specific course or subject, and their complete course and textbook are available digitally.

These courses and other student guidance materials are developed by a large and devoted team of professors, developers, and designers at SMU.

Easy to Learn

SMU distance courses are easy to learn as they are available at students’ fingertips. Once they get admitted to the course, they have access to the entire syllabus, and they can learn their course according to their time preferences and schedule.

These courses are flexible yet affordable for every group of students. Students get access to the digital study material with the pre-recorded videos and others on the go.

Awarded and recognized

Students will be pursuing their distance courses from the awarded and recognized university. Distance courses are approved by Indian authorities, and they are recognizable all over the world.

Their ranking is among the top 8 universities in the top private engineering colleges, the top 20 ranking in the top India colleges of engineering, the 21st ranking in job placements, and the 24th in course designing, guidance, and support.

24 Hours Support

Students get access to the two-way communication setup, which helps them solve their queries, and students can ask their questions to clear their doubts. They will get the best solution to their problem easily through these 24/7 help desks.

A Wide Range of Courses

Currently, SMU provides graduation and post-graduation courses for BBA, BCA, MBA, and MCA students.

These courses have a wide range of specializations, which leads to the placement industry opening a lot of job opportunities after completion of the courses.

These four degrees are considered among the top courses to pursue after class 12 and graduation.

Courses SMU Offers

The SMU distance learning program offers four courses: MBA, BBA, BCA, and MCA. Explore these courses in detail:

Bachelor’s of Business Administration (BBA)

SMU’s distance BBA program is a 3-year undergraduate degree program for students who want to pursue a career in the field of business and management. Students get access to these materials just after admission, after which they are independent because they do not need to attend the traditional classroom program. They can study their course at their own pace and at their convenience. The syllabus of these distance courses is updated according to the latest course needs and requirements. This BBA degree helps students become skilled and knowledgeable

Eligibility

  • For this course, students need to complete their 10+2 in the field of commerce or any other stream, or a 3-year diploma degree.

Scope

  • After completion of this course, you will have great job opportunities in the relevant field with a handsome salary package from the beginning of your career.

Fees Structure

  • 16,250 INR

Semester

  • This course is divided into six semesters
Master’s of Business Administration (MBA)

SMU’s distance MBA program is a 2-year post graduation program, which is one of the most demanding courses to be done by students after completion of the graduation degree in any field. SMU provides pre-recorded lectures and other study materials to the students at their convenience so that they can study according to their time and preferences.

Eligibility

  • To pursue an MBA distance course, students need to complete their graduation in any field from a recognized university, either in a traditional classroom training program or through distance learning.

Scope

  • As it is said that the MBA degree is the most in-demand, every second student chooses to pursue this degree just because of the scope it provides after completing the course. After an MBA course, students are opened to a lot of opportunities, which provides them with better chances to enhance their careers and grow in their desired fields.

Fees Structure

  • semester-wise fees are 21,250 INR

Semester

  • This course is divided into 4 semesters

Specialization

  • Marketing Management
  • Information System Management
  • Human Resource Management
  • Financial Management
Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)

Students who pursue a BCA course are those who are interested in the fields of computer science and IT. BCA distance course helps students to guide them in the fields of advanced programming, coding languages, and other important computing fields. SMU provides the best of the pre-recorded lectures and a healthy studying environment to their distance education learners so that they don’t miss any important topics and get chances to enhance their capability, skills, and knowledge in their chosen field.

Eligibility

  • To pursue this particular course, students need to complete their 10+2 with mathematics and computer science as their optional subjects in their chosen stream

Scope

  • After completing a BCA degree, students get a lot of job opportunities related to their specialization. SMU collaborates with the best business industries to provide placement after these courses. Students can anticipate a handsome salary package when they get placed through the college.

Fees Structure

  • Semester-wise fees are 16,250 INR

Semester

  • This course is divided into 6 semesters
Master’s of Computer Application (MCA)

SMU provides an MCA distance course for students who seek a professional course in the field of computer science. Students get digital textbooks, pre-recorded lectures, and other study materials after enrolment. Students need to access their entire course, and they can re-read their lessons anytime, according to their time.

Eligibility

  • After completing a graduation degree in any field, they get access to enroll themselves in this distance learning program. However, BCA graduates are preferred first when it comes to counseling students.

Scope

  • After completing the distance MCA course from the SMU distance learning program, students are opened to a lot of job opportunities where they get a lot of exposure to the field of computer science and other related fields.

Semester

  • This course is divided into 4 semesters

Fees Structure

  • Semester-wise fees are 18,250 INR

Concluding Remarks!

SMU distance learning programs are the best to pursue for students who are stuck in other professional commitments but want to continue their education to enhance their skills and be professionals in their respective fields. SMU graduates have a placement record in their relevant fields. To learn more about SMU and register, visit the C-DEC official website.
