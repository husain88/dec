Virtual MBA -Recognised in India

Yes, the online MBA degree programmes in India are accredited by the university grant commission (UGC) and the distance education bureau (DEB). If you are seeking admission to an online MBA programme, you must first determine whether the university is UGC recognised.

Distance MBA-Regular MBA-Notable difference

  • The main difference between an online MBA and a traditional MBA is that everything takes place virtually. The full-time MBA programme has a set and rigid timetable for assignment submission, class participation, and exam dates, whereas online MBA programmes have a very flexible teaching-learning environment.
  • In the case of an online MBA course, the candidate does not have to commit the entire course period only to the degree and can work full-time during this time, which is one of the key reasons why students pick an online MBA degree when weighing the benefits of offline vs online MBA.
