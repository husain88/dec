Mizoram University Online Programs

Mizoram University (MZU) is a central university located in Aizawl, Mizoram. MZU is a UGC-DEB approved university & comes at 78th position among top Universities of India in NIRF Ranking. The university has been accredited with the A grade by the NAAC.

Central universities are public universities in India, established by the ACT of Parliament under the purview of the Department of Higher Education of the Ministry of Education. 

Is pursuing your Degree from MZU online Valid?

If you’re planning to continue your studies, online Mizoram Central University can be a great option for you. These online programs offer career growth chances for working professionals/fresh graduates who wish to upskill their prospects in their domain.

Mizoram University online course admissions process takes place every year in two sessions, January and July. Eligible candidates can apply through their official website including paying their application fee of INR 200. The admission process is based on non-entrance exams, however, admission for professional degrees like MBA  are purely based on state, national, or university entrance exams like MAT or CAT.

Mizoram University online offers certificate, executive, bachelor’s, and master’s-level programs for various professional fields such as app development, general management, Internet of Things (IoT), BCom, MBA, and more.
