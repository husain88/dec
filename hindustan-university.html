Hindustan University Online Programs

Hindustan Online-Centre for Open and Digital Education (CODE) is a part of prestigious Hindustan group of institution that offers higher education to the students. The University was established in 1985 as a Deemed to be University & is affiliated by the UGC & ranked Grade ‘A’ by NAAC.
